Risk Assessment

Risks & Mitigations

HIGH RISK
Mobile Safari Background Tabs
Safari throttles timers in background tabs. Mitigation: use requestAnimationFrame with visibilitychange recovery, persist timer state on every tick.
MEDIUM RISK
Theme FOUC on Load
Flash of unstyled content when theme loads from localStorage. Mitigation: inline blocking script in head applies theme class before first paint.
MEDIUM RISK
Bundle Size Creep
Educational content + SVG clocks could push past performance budget. Mitigation: lazy-load panels, inline critical SVG only, CI size gate.
LOW RISK
localStorage Quota
User preferences + timer state could approach 5MB limit on some browsers. Mitigation: structured cleanup on version migration, compression.
